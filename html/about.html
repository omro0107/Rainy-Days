About us

Rainydays was founded in 1997 and are based in Oslo, where we have our flagship store. We offer a wide selection of high-quality rain jackets designed for active outdoor enthusiasts like you. We are committed to providing products that not only protect you from rain and wind, but also environmentally conscious. That's why we have carefully chosen rain jackets from manufacturers who prioritize sustainability and eco-friendly materials.

We offer a wide range of styles, colors, and sizes, so you can find the perfect jacket that suits your style and needs. Whether you're out on an adventure, commuting to work, or simply want to be prepared for unexpected rain showers, we have the jacket for you. We take pride in offering high-quality products and exceptional customer service.

Take a look at our collection, and join our community of satisfied customers who always stay dry!

We care

The textile industry accounts for almost 10% of the world's greenhouse gas emissions. This amounts to 1.2 billion tonnes of CO2. It is more than both air and ship traffic combined. Every year, the clothing industry consumes 93 billion cubic meters of water. This corresponds to the water consumption of 5 million people. We focus on sustainable materials, and want to minimize our footprint on the planet.

Five percent of our profit goes to climate compensation. It is also possible to exchange your old Rainydays clothes in our stores and get a voucher in return.
